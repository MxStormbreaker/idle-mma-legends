Privacy Policy — Idle MMA Legends

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Idle MMA Legends ("the app," "the game") is a free idle/incremental mobile game. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and how it's handled.

Account & Sign-In

The app uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create your account automatically when you first open it — there's no sign-up form, no email address, and no password required to play. This assigns your device a randomly generated ID used to save your progress.

Optionally linking a Google Account (when available) associates your Google email address with your save data, solely to let you restore your progress on a new device. This is optional and not required to play the game.

Gameplay Data We Store

To save your progress, the app stores gameplay data tied to your account ID, including:

  • Fighter stats, level, career progress, and prestige history
  • In-game currency and item/gear inventory
  • Cosmetic and companion/relationship progress
  • A display name you choose (does not need to be your real name)

Social & Shared Data

If you join an in-game "Gym" (a social/league feature), certain data becomes visible to other players in the same gym or on public leaderboards, including:

  • Your chosen display name and gym role
  • Your fighter's power level, career tier, and weekly activity/contribution
  • An optional short status message you can set, visible to other players

This data is stored to enable shared features like leaderboards, gym rosters, and gym-wide votes, and is visible to other users of the app as part of normal gameplay — it is not sold or shared with third parties.

Cross-Device Sync

The app offers an optional "Sync Code" feature to move your save between devices. If you generate a sync code, a snapshot of your save is stored and can be retrieved by anyone who has that code — treat it like a password and don't share it.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not display third-party advertisements.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not process real-money payments at this time. (If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated before any payment feature is enabled.)

Data Storage & Security

Gameplay data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore Database). Some data (like your active save) is also stored locally on your device. Firebase's own data handling is governed by Google's Firebase privacy policy.

Data Deletion

Since accounts are anonymous by default, uninstalling the app and clearing its data effectively removes your local save. If you've linked a Google account or generated a Sync Code and want associated cloud data deleted, contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and is not designed for a young children's audience.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app's features change. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
